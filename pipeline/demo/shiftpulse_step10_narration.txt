ShiftPulse is a local operations handoff board built from one request. It starts with seven records and immediately shows four items needing attention. The incoming supervisor filters to urgent work, reducing the queue to three priorities. Each record keeps its owner, status, and exact next action visible. Here, the cooling-line issue is marked resolved. The board responds instantly: attention needed drops from four to three, and resolved work rises from two to three. Clearing the filter restores the complete queue with every owner and next action intact. From input to output, ShiftPulse turns scattered shift notes into a focused, accountable handoff, running locally with fictional data and no external services.
