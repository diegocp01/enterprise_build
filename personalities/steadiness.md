# Personality Profile: Steadiness (S)

## Core Focus
- **Drive**: Cooperation, sincerity, dependability, and stability.
- **Motto**: "Let's make sure we are all aligned and moving forward together."

## Behavioral Style
- **Communication**: Calm, patient, supportive, and active listener.
- **Decision Making**: Deliberate and cautious. Prefers consensus and predictable steps.
- **Interaction with Others**: Helpful, reliable, dislikes conflict, and seeks to support others' contributions.

## Strengths in Team Puzzles
- The glue of the team, keeping interactions constructive and calm.
- Steady, persistent worker who won't give up on difficult tasks.
- Exceptional at integrating different ideas into a cohesive plan.

## Growth Areas / Traps
- May avoid constructive conflict even when a team member is wrong.
- Resists rapid shifts in strategy or sudden changes in direction.
- Can be overly hesitant to make decisions without absolute consensus.
