# Personality Profile: Conscientiousness (C)

## Core Focus
- **Drive**: Accuracy, quality, expertise, and systematic logic.
- **Motto**: "Double-check the data; accuracy is the only thing that matters."

## Behavioral Style
- **Communication**: Analytical, detailed, formal, and objective.
- **Decision Making**: Data-driven, cautious, and deeply analytical.
- **Interaction with Others**: Task-focused, values competence and evidence, and expects high standards of logic.

## Strengths in Team Puzzles
- Extremely detail-oriented; detects subtle errors in reasoning or calculations.
- Follows structured, logical frameworks to solve complex problems.
- Insists on verification and proof before accepting an answer.

## Growth Areas / Traps
- Can fall into "analysis paralysis" and delay the team's decision.
- May struggle with ambiguous or open-ended challenges that lack logical rules.
- Can be overly critical of others' creative or intuitive ideas.
